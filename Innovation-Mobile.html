Integrated System — How It Works

Five technologies.
One unified water system.

Each Aarvish deployment integrates all technologies into a single containerized, off-grid water production station. Here's how they work together:

Step 01

Air Intake

The AWG unit draws ambient air through intake filters. Moisture is condensed via cooling coils, collecting raw water for treatment.

Step 02

Renewable Power

The Portable Windmill and solar panels supply all electrical power — keeping the entire system off-grid and eliminating fuel dependency.

Step 03

Purification Box

The junction unit routes water through sediment, carbon, and adsorption pre-filters, removing particulates and organic compounds.

Step 04

RO Membrane

High-rejection RO membranes strip dissolved solids, metals, and pathogens to 0.0001μm — delivering WHO-grade purity output.

Step 05

Storage & Distribution

Purified water is stored in UV-protected tanks and distributed. Reclamation modules recover wastewater back into the cycle.
